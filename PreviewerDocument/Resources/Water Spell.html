Getting Started with the Water Preview

In this tutorial, you'll learn about the Preview, a Preview that casts water out of the wand. It's a very handy Preview that may even save lives.

Getting Started

The Water Preview , also known as the Preview, is a Preview used to generate water. The Preview can be used to save lives in life threatening situations. The water generated can be drunk and can be used to extinguish fire. The caster may control the direction and quantity of water being produced.

Incantation: Aquarius
Pronunciation: uh-kweh-ree-uhs
Type: Preview
Light:Blue
Description: Creates water
Etymology: From latin means "water bearer"
Hand movement: Point wand at target
